Letter of Recognition


All requests for recognition from the Executive Office of the Governor should be submitted at least 1 week prior to the date recognition is needed.

Please remember that recognition requests are reviewed on a case-by-case basis and the Governor's Office reserves the right to decline any request.

For questions, please call the Executive Office of the Governor - Constituent Relations Division at (517) 335-7858.
